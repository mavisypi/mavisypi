Act on it



Brazil is a tropical country, reaching impressive 40 °C on a hot summer day. Personagens Vivos, or Costume Characters in Brazil work for several hours on end under extreme heat conditions.They get paid minimum wage and have poor working conditions.

Testimonial

“The bad part is also the heat, because I work at lunchtime, from 11 am to 2 pm, and in the heart of São Paulo. You sweat, you hear people cursing...”

"I suffer a lot of physical aggression from people, they hit me hard, kick me, punch me, hurt me, push me, [...] they think they can put the aggression out because they are in a dark place."
